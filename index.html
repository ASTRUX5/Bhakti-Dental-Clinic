Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes! Walk-ins are welcome during working hours (Mon-Sat: 9 AM-1 PM, 5-9 PM). However, booking an appointment ensures minimal waiting time.

Is parking available at the clinic?

Yes, free parking is available near the clinic. You can park conveniently on Vastral Road.

Are treatments painful?

We specialize in painless dentistry using modern techniques and local anesthesia. Most patients report feeling minimal to no discomfort during procedures.

What should I bring for my first visit?

Just bring any previous dental records or X-rays if available. For kids, bring their favorite toy or book to keep them comfortable. No other documents required.

Are kids comfortable during treatment?

Absolutely! Dr. Pratik is specially trained in pediatric dentistry. We create a friendly environment with gentle techniques to ensure children feel safe and comfortable.

How long does a typical appointment take?

Consultations take 15-30 minutes. Simple procedures like cleaning take 30-45 minutes. Complex treatments like RCT or implants may require 1-2 hours depending on the case.
